VANILLA CUPCAKES - SOFT, SWEET AND SIMPLE.

A light, fluffy cupcake recipe with a buttery vanilla flavor. Perfect for birthdays, snacks, or a cozy baking afternoon.

RECIPE DETAILS

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 18-22 minutes

Servings: 12 Cupcakes

Difficulty: Easy

INGREDIENTS

  • 11/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Cupcake liners
  • Frosting of your choice

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 3500F or 1750C. Line a 12-cup muffin tin with cupcake liners
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl: flour, baking powder, and salt. Set the bowl aside.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar in a separate bowl until the mixture looks pale and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each egg. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients by adding the flour mixture and milk in small amounts, alternating
    between the two. Mix until just combined
  6. Fill the cupcake liners about 2/3 full so the cupcakes have room to rise.
  7. Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

TIPS/NOTES

For extra-soft cupcakes, do not overmix the batter once the flour is added. You can top them with vanilla buttercream, chocolate frosting, sprinkles, fruit, or a tiny dusting of powdered sugar. Viola! Your cupcake is ready!!
